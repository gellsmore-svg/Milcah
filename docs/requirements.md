# Project Requirements — Coherence Engine v0.2

## FR1 — Framework Ingestion

Accept:

* books
* documents
* hypotheses
* argument trees
* conversations
* web research
* structured ontology

---

## FR2 — Reasoning Extraction

Extract:

* claims
* observations
* assumptions
* commitments
* bridge claims
* enthymemes
* dependencies
* conclusions

---

## FR3 — Ontology Construction

Construct worldview trees.

For each concept determine:

* parent
* children
* dependencies
* bridge relationships
* placement confidence

Track:

* resolved
* unresolved
* orphaned
* contradictory nodes

---

## FR4 — Recursive Reasoning

For every node ask:

* what supports this?
* what must be true?
* what does this imply?
* what assumptions exist?
* what explains those assumptions?

Store recursive units.

No fixed recursion depth.

---

## FR5 — Counter-Framework Research

Generate:

* alternative explanations
* competing frameworks
* adjacent interpretations
* strongest objections

Support web retrieval.

Research should be hypothesis-driven.

---

## FR6 — Logical Fallacy Analysis

Evaluate every reasoning step.

Examples:

* contradiction
* circularity
* equivocation
* special pleading
* appeal to popularity
* appeal to authority
* category error
* false equivalence
* unsupported bridge
* hidden commitment

Store locations.

---

## FR7 — Explanatory Debt

Track:

* assumption count
* bridge count
* unresolved ontology
* recursion depth
* dependency load

---

## FR8 — Burden Symmetry

Apply identical challenge structures to all frameworks.

No exemptions.

---

## FR9 — Coherence Metrics

Track separately:

* local coherence
* global coherence
* explanatory breadth
* ontological completeness
* fracture density
* uncertainty burden

Do not include:

* popularity
* confidence
* institutional acceptance

---

## FR10 — Iterative Refinement

Store per reasoning unit:

* scores
* ontology state
* fractures
* assumptions
* unresolved nodes
* trend over time

---

## FR11 — Terminal Conditions

End rounds when:

* convergence reached
* recursion threshold met
* repeated objection patterns
* human review requested
* compute budget exhausted

Long-running analysis supported.
