# Concepts

Grounded in [`docs/philosophy.md`](https://github.com/gellsmore-svg/Milcah/blob/main/docs/philosophy.md),
[`docs/requirements.md`](https://github.com/gellsmore-svg/Milcah/blob/main/docs/requirements.md),
and [`docs/architecture.md`](https://github.com/gellsmore-svg/Milcah/blob/main/docs/architecture.md).

- **[Coherence engine](coherence-engine.md)** — judges coherence, not truth;
  improves the honesty and completeness of inquiry.
- **[Reasoning units](reasoning-units.md)** — the typed units reasoning is broken
  into (FR2).
- **[Burden symmetry](burden-symmetry.md)** — equal explanatory burden for every
  framework; forced certainty is forbidden.
- **[Recursive pressure-testing](recursive-pressure-testing.md)** — the five
  questions applied to every node, to convergence (FR4/FR11).
- **[Sibling integration](sibling-integration.md)** — Milcah stands on Tirzah,
  Mahalath, Hoglah, and Cairn.
